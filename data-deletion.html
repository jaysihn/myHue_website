How to Delete Your Data in myHue

myHue stores only the following data:

  • Your display name, email, and password (for account creation and login)
  • Photos you have used in the app
  • Analysis results from your photos

To delete all app data:

  1. Open the app and go to the User Account page.
  2. Tap Clear All App Data to remove your account information, photos, and analysis results from your device.
